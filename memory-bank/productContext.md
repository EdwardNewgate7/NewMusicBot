# Product Context: Harmony Music Bot

## Why this project exists
The Harmony Music Bot is designed for Telegram groups to provide a premium, stable, and feature-rich music and video playback experience. It aims to replace unreliable or complex bots with a seamless UI and high-quality streaming.

## Problems it solves
- Unreliable playback in Telegram voice chats.
- Difficulties in downloading media from various platforms (Instagram, TikTok, etc.).
- Lack of engagement in groups (solved by the scoring and tagging system).
- Age/region restrictions on YouTube (solved by cookie.txt support).

## How it should work
- Users send `/oynat` or `/indir`.
- The bot searches/detects the media.
- The bot downloads the high-quality 320kbps audio or 720p video.
- The assistant joins the voice chat and starts streaming.
- Admin controls allow skipping, pausing, and managing the queue.

## User Experience Goals
- "Wow" factor with custom emojis and colorful buttons.
- Fast response times.
- 24/7 reliability for long listening sessions.
